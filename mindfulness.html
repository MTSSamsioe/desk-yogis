Guided meditations

Being mindful can have many advantages some of which you can read about here. When you have been concentrating for a prolonged period of time your performance drop significantly. Therefore taking a 5 or 10 minute guided meditation will help clear your head, increasing focus and reliveing stress. Below you will find two guided meditations from Goodful that you could do right at your desk.
