Sitting in front of your screen for long durations can take a toll on your body. It is easy to forget about your posture and slouch straining your back and neck. Take a 5-10 minute guided meditation


The average adult can concentrate effectively for 40-45 minutes. Therefore it is important to take a break now and then to avoid misstakes and tedious redoing of tasks. To make the most of your break it is beneficial to follow a 5-10 minute guided meditation.


  • Some of the benefits can be:

  • Gaining a new perspective on stressful situations
  • Building skills to manage your stress
  • Increasing self-awareness
  • Focusing on the present
  • Reducing negative emotions
  • Increasing imagination and creativity
  • Lowering resting heart rate
  • Lowering resting blood pressure
