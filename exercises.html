Toes, Back, Hips & Legs exercises

Lower back, hips & Legs exercises

Flex & Point toes

Position: Sit with straight back and legs on the edge of your seat with heals on the floor.

  • Flex your feet towards you hold for 5 seconds
  • Flex your feet towards the floor, hold for 5 seconds
  • Repeat 3 times

Tippy toes stretch

Position: Sit on your chair with your legs on an 90 degree angle and your sole of your feet on the ground.

  • Rais your heels from the floor as high as you can and hold for 5 seconds
  • Lower heels back to the floor leave for 2 seconds
  • Repeat 3 times

Calves stretches

Position: Sit straight and put one ankle on the opposite knee.

  • Push your top knee down with your hand on the same side and hold for 3 deep breaths
  • Switch leg and repeat on the opposite leg
  • Repeat 2 times on each leg
  • For a deeper stretch lean forward
