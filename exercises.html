Lower back, hips & Legs exercises


Toes, Back, Hips & Legs exercises

Flex & Point toes


Position: Sit with straight back and legs on the edge of your seat with heals on the floor.


  • Flex your feet towards you hold for 5 seconds
  • Flex your feet towards the floor, hold for 5 seconds
  • Repeat 3 times

Tippy toes stretch


Position: Sit on your chair with your legs on an 90 degree angle and your sole of your feet on the ground.


  • Rais your heels from the floor as high as you can and hold for 5 seconds
  • Lower heels back to the floor leave for 2 seconds
  • Repeat 3 times

Calves stretches


Position: Sit straight and put one ankle on the opposite knee.


  • Push your top knee down with your hand on the same side and hold for 3 deep breaths
  • Switch leg and repeat on the opposite leg
  • Repeat 2 times on each leg
  • For a deeper stretch lean forward


Hips & Lower Back

Seated spinal twist


Position: Sit on your chair with your palms together close to your chest amd lean forward slightly.


  • Twist your body so you can put your elbow on your opposite knee and hold for three deep breaths.
  • Twist your body to the opposite side and hold
  • Repeat 3 times

Hamstrings, Shoulders & Back


Position:Sit with wide legs, soles on the floor, hands on your knees and lean forward.


  • Move one shoulder towards the center of your body twisting your back and shoulders, hold for 5 seconds.
  • Switch shoulder and rotate in the opposite direction and, hold for 5 seconds.
  • Repeat 2 times on each side

Revers happy baby


Position:Sit on your chair with soles on the floor.


  • Bend forward
  • Put elbows behind calves and reach toes
  • Hold for 3 deep breaths

Seated forward bend


Position:Sit on your chair with soles on the floor.


  • Lean forward and push torso into your thighs
  • Reach your hands to the floor
  • Hold for three deep breaths


Shoulders, Back & Neck


Upper back stretches

Back arch


Position:Sit on your chair with soles on the floor.


  • Inhale and arch your back backwards hold for 10 seconds
  • Exhale and round your back forward, hold for 10 seconds
  • Repet 10 times

Seated spinal twist


Position:Sit on your chair with a straight back and your soles on the floor.


  • Rotate your shoulders 90 degrees and hold for 10 seconds
  • Rotate your shoulders in the opposite direction and hold for 10 seconds
  • Repeat 2 times on each side

Interlacefingers & arch back


Position:Sit on your chair with a straight back and your soles on the floor.


  • Interlace your fingers behind your back and arch backwards, hold for 10 seconds
  • Repeat 2 times

Side stretch


Position:Sit on your chair with a straight back and your soles on the floor.


  • Reach one arm over your ear and lean to that side and feel a stretch in your the side of your body, hold for 10 seconds
  • Reach the opposite arm to the other side, hold for 10 seconds
  • Repeat both sides 2 times

Upper back stretch


Position:Place your elbows on the desk infront of you while sitting down on your chair and lean forward slightly.


  • Lower your head and back in between your elbows, hold for 10 seconds
  • Repeat 2 times

Neck stretches

Neck vertical stretch


Position:Sit on your chair and interlace your fingers behind your head.


  • Push your head down and lean forward to feel a stretch in your neck and upper back, hold for 10 seconds
  • Arch your neck and backwards, hold for 10 seconds
  • Repeat 2 times

Neck side stretch


Position:Sit straight on your chair with soles on the floor.


  • Use your arm to gently pull your head to the side to feel a stretch in your neck, hold for 10 seconds
  • Use arm on opposite side to pull head in the other direction, hold for 10 seconds
  • Repeat 2 times
